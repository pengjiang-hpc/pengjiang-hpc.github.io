## About Me
I am an assistant professor in the Computer Science Department at the [University of Iowa](https://cs.uiowa.edu/). My research interests generally lie in the areas of high-performance computing, systems, and machine learning. My current research focuses on providing compiler and runtime support for randomized data-intensive applications on heterogeneous hardware. I am a recipient of the NSF CAREER Award. 

I earned my Ph.D. degree from the [Ohio State University](https://cse.osu.edu/) under the supervision of [Dr. Gagan Agrawal](https://www.linkedin.com/in/gaganagrawal2). I received an M.S. in Computer Science from [Chinese Academy of Science](http://english.ict.cas.cn/) and a B.S. from [Xidian University](https://en.xidian.edu.cn/), Xi'an, China.
