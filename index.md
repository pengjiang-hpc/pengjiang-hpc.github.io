## About Me
I am an Assistant Professor in the Computer Science Department at the University of Iowa. 

I am interested in exploring new programming techniques to simplify the development of high-performance and parallel programs for data science, machine learning, and scientific computing applications. I received the NSF CAREER Award for research in compiler and runtime support for data-intensive applications on heterogeneous hardware. 

Check out our Python-based programming tool: [Cuke](https://cuke.dev/pengjiang-hpc/cuke_demo/).
